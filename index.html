Menu

We offer a variety of different options throughout the week, ranging from different cuisines. Fresh, convenient grab-and-go meals made with all natural ingredients. Breakfast, lunch and dinner options – everything you need to eat healthy all day long.

Taste our hand-crafted pizza made with all-natural ingredients, our signature hearty red sauce and quattro fromaggio blend cheese. Stop in for a sweet treat. Wholesome baked goods delivered daily and never frozen, including our signature muffin in six different flavours. Everything on the menu is $5 and under guaranteed!

About

At 25th Street Market Cafe, it's the little things that are important to us – from the way we buy our food to the wholesome way we prepare it.

We make it easy to enjoy good food without compromising on quality, choice or convenience. Make sure to try all of our signature recipes – a creative collection of unique and delicious food offerings.
